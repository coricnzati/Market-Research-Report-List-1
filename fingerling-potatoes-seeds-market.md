In contrast, micro-propagation involves advanced techniques like tissue culture to rapidly produce disease-free seed varieties in a controlled environment. This method results in higher yields and uniform quality, catering to the growing demand for innovative agricultural solutions and ensuring sustainable production.</p></p>
