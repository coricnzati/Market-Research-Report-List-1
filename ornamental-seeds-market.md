<p><strong>The Ornamental Seeds Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Annual Seeds</li><li>Biennial Seeds</li><li>Perennial Seeds</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ornamental seeds market is categorized into three main types: annual, biennial, and perennial seeds. Annual seeds complete their life cycle in one year, offering vibrant blooms for seasonal displays. Biennial seeds grow for two years, producing foliage in the first year and flowers in the second, suitable for creating lasting garden structures. Perennial seeds, on the other hand, thrive for multiple years, providing continuous growth and blossoms, making them ideal for long-term landscaping and maintenance.</p></p>
