<p><p>The Frozen Fresh Meat Packaging market primarily utilizes two advanced packaging methods: vacuum packaging and modified atmosphere packaging (MAP). Vacuum packaging involves sealing meat in airtight bags to eliminate oxygen, which helps prevent spoilage and preserve freshness. In contrast, MAP replaces the air inside the packaging with a controlled mix of gases, extending shelf life while maintaining quality. Both methods enhance the preservation of frozen meat, improving safety and convenience for consumers and reducing waste in the supply chain.</p></p>
