<p><p>The Emergency Exit Sign market is categorized into two main types: electrical and non-electrical exit signs. Electrical exit signs utilize light sources, such as LEDs or fluorescent lamps, to enhance visibility in low-light conditions, ensuring timely evacuation during emergencies. In contrast, non-electrical exit signs rely on photoluminescent or traditional materials that glow in the dark or use reflective surfaces, often functioning independently of power sources. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring safety and compliance in commercial and public spaces.</p></p>
