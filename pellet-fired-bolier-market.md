<p><p>The pellet-fired boiler market comprises three main types: fully-automatic, semi-automatic, and manual wood pellet boilers. Fully-automatic boilers offer advanced features, including automated fuel feeding and ignition, ensuring efficient operation with minimal user input. Semi-automatic boilers require some manual intervention for fuel loading but automate other processes like ignition and temperature control. Manual wood pellet boilers necessitate significant user involvement for loading and operation, making them more suited for users seeking a hands-on approach to heating.</p></p>
