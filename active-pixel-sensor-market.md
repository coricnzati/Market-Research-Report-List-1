<p><p>The Active-Pixel Sensor (APS) market includes various technologies like Buried Photodiode Structure, Selection Transistor, and MOS Amplifier. Buried photodiodes enhance sensitivity by reducing optical crosstalk and improving light absorption, while selection transistors enable pixel-level readout, optimizing performance and reducing noise. MOS amplifiers are integrated to boost signal strength, facilitating better image quality. Together, these components create advanced image sensors for applications in smartphones, cameras, and other imaging devices, driving innovation and performance in the imaging market.</p></p>
