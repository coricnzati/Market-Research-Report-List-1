<p><p>The Implantable Cardiac Stimulators market comprises various types, including Implantable Cardioverter Defibrillators (ICDs), Pacemakers, Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy Defibrillators (CRT-D), and Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy Pacemakers (CRT-P). ICDs are designed to detect and correct life-threatening arrhythmias. Pacemakers regulate the heart's rhythm in patients with bradycardia. CRT-D devices synchronize heartbeats to improve efficiency and deliver shock therapy if needed. CRT-P devices also synchronize heartbeats but do not provide defibrillation. Each type addresses specific cardiac conditions, enhancing patient outcomes.</p></p>
