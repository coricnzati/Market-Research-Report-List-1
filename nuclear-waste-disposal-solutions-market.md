<p><ul><li>Low Level Waste</li><li>Medium Level Waste</li><li>High Level Waste</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nuclear waste disposal solutions are categorized into three market types based on waste radioactivity levels. Low-level waste (LLW) includes items like clothing and tools, requiring minimal shielding and managed through near-surface disposal methods. Medium-level waste (MLW), containing higher radiation levels, may need engineered storage solutions and some shielding. High-level waste (HLW), such as spent nuclear fuel, is highly radioactive and necessitates deep geological repositories for long-term management, ensuring isolation for thousands of years to protect human health and the environment.</p></p>
