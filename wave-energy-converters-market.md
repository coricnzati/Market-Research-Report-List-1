<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Wave Energy Converters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Point Absorbers Devices</li><li>Terminators Devices</li><li>Attenuators Devices</li><li>Oscillating Wave Surge Devices</li><li>Submerged Pressure Differential Devices</li><li>Rotating Mass Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wave Energy Converters (WECs) encompass various technologies harnessing ocean energy. Point Absorbers capture buoy motion in waves, while Terminators absorb energy at the shoreline. Attenuators are long, floating structures that sway with waves, generating power along their length. Oscillating Wave Surge Devices convert surge motion into energy, and Submerged Pressure Differential Devices exploit pressure changes underwater. Lastly, Rotating Mass Devices utilize the kinetic energy of rotating masses to generate electricity. Each type reflects unique designs tailored for specific wave conditions and energy extraction.</p></p>
