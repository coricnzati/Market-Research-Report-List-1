<p><p>In the B2C market, augmented reality (AR) glasses are categorized into monocular and binocular types. Monocular AR glasses feature a single display lens, allowing users to overlay digital content in one eye, making them lightweight and versatile for applications like simple navigation or notifications. Binocular AR glasses, on the other hand, incorporate dual displays, providing a more immersive experience by enabling 3D visuals and spatial awareness, ideal for gaming, education, and interactive retail experiences, enhancing user engagement and interactivity.</p></p>
