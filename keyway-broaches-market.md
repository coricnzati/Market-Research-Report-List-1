<p><p>The keyway broaches market comprises three main types: semicircular, flat, and wedge broaches. Semicircular broaches are utilized for creating curved keyways, often found in machinery where smooth rotational movement is required. Flat broaches cut rectangular keyways and are commonly used in mechanical applications needing precise alignment. Wedge broaches are designed for tapered keyways, allowing for easy insertion and locking of components. Each type serves distinct functions and caters to varied industrial applications, ensuring efficient and effective production processes.</p></p>
