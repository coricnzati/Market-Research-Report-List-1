<p><strong>The Outdoor Mist Cooling System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Pressure Outdoor Mist Cooling System</li><li>Medium Pressure Outdoor Mist Cooling System</li><li>High Pressure Outdoor Mist Cooling System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Outdoor mist cooling systems are classified into three types based on pressure levels. Low-pressure systems operate at around 50 PSI, ideal for residential use and small patios, providing gentle humidity. Medium-pressure systems, ranging from 100 to 400 PSI, are suitable for commercial spaces, offering enhanced cooling with a fine mist. High-pressure systems, exceeding 800 PSI, create ultra-fine droplets that cool large outdoor areas efficiently and are often used in industrial settings, sports venues, and large events for maximum comfort.</p></p>
