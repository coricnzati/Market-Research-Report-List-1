<p><p>Firestop sprayable mastic markets can be categorized into two main types: one component and two component systems. One component mastic is ready to use, requiring no mixing, making it convenient for quick applications. In contrast, two component mastic involves a mixing process before application, offering enhanced performance and adhesion properties. Both types are designed to seal penetrations in fire-rated walls and floors, providing crucial fire protection by preventing the spread of smoke and flames through openings.</p></p>
