<p><strong>The Grain Drying Equipments Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Cross Flow"</li><li>"Mixed Flow"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The grain drying equipment market is primarily categorized into two types: cross flow and mixed flow systems. Cross flow dryers feature a horizontal airflow that passes through the grain vertically, allowing for uniform moisture removal. These systems are efficient for small to medium-sized operations. In contrast, mixed flow dryers combine both vertical and horizontal airflow, optimizing drying performance and efficiency. This versatility makes them suitable for larger operations, effectively handling varying grain types and capacities while ensuring consistent drying results.</p></p>
