<p><strong>The Iron Casting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gray iron</li><li>Ductile iron</li><li>Malleable iron</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The iron casting market comprises three main types: gray iron, ductile iron, and malleable iron. Gray iron, known for its excellent castability and machinability, is widely used in automotive and machinery components. Ductile iron, characterized by its enhanced strength and ductility, finds applications in piping and heavy-duty components. Malleable iron, produced by heat-treating white iron, offers high tensile strength and is commonly used in fittings and hardware. Each type serves distinct industries based on their specific mechanical properties.</p></p>
