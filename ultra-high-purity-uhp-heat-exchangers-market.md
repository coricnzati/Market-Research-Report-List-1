<p><p>The Ultra High Purity (UHP) heat exchangers market is primarily divided into two types: shell and tube type and immersion type. Shell and tube UHP heat exchangers consist of a series of tubes enclosed in a shell, optimizing heat transfer while minimizing contamination risks, making them suitable for semiconductor and pharmaceutical applications. Immersion type UHP heat exchangers involve direct immersion of heating or cooling coils in a process fluid, allowing efficient thermal regulation with reduced space requirements and increased cleanliness.</p></p>
