<p><p>The car immobilizer market is categorized into Type I and Type II segments based on their technology and application. Type I immobilizers rely on traditional methods, such as key-based systems, which use physical keys to start the vehicle. In contrast, Type II immobilizers incorporate advanced technologies, including RFID and biometric systems, providing enhanced security. These systems disable the engine unless the correct credential is present, making it harder for thieves to bypass and ensuring better vehicle protection overall.</p></p>
