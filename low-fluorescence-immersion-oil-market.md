<p><strong>The Low Fluorescence Immersion Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Medium Viscosity</li><li>Low Viscosity</li><li>High Viscosity</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Low Fluorescence Immersion Oil market is segmented into three types based on viscosity: medium, low, and high. Medium viscosity oils offer a balanced performance for various microscopy applications, providing good refractive index and immersion properties. Low viscosity oils are designed for enhanced fluidity, enabling easier penetration in fine specimens, but may have lower refractive indices. High viscosity oils, on the other hand, provide increased thickness and stability, ensuring optimal immersion but can be more challenging to apply. Each type serves specific microscopy needs, influencing image clarity and precision.</p></p>
