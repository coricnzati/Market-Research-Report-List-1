<p><p>The medical digital thermometer market encompasses two main types: electronic and infrared thermometers. Electronic thermometers use electronic sensors to provide accurate temperature readings and are suitable for oral, rectal, or underarm use, offering rapid results. Infrared thermometers, on the other hand, measure temperature from a distance by detecting thermal radiation, making them ideal for quick assessments, especially in non-contact scenarios. Both types cater to diverse healthcare settings, emphasizing accuracy and efficiency in temperature monitoring.</p></p>
