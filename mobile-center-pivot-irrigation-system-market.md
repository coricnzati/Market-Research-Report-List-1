<p><p>The Mobile Center Pivot Irrigation System Market consists of several key components. Pivot Points allow the system to rotate around a central point. Control Panels manage the irrigation process, adjusting settings as needed. Spans are the lengthy arms that extend outward for distribution, while Sprinkler Drops deliver water directly to crops. Tower Drive Wheels facilitate movement, ensuring smooth navigation across fields, and the Drive Train connects the wheels to the power source, providing necessary propulsion and control for efficient operation.</p></p>
