<p><p>Balloon expanding and self-expanding stents are medical devices used to treat narrowed or blocked blood vessels. Additionally, the growing demand for minimally invasive procedures is propelling the adoption of these devices. Innovations such as drug-eluting stents, which release medication to prevent restenosis, are also driving market expansion. </p><p>The Balloon Expanding and Self-Expanding Stents Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.2% during the forecast period.
