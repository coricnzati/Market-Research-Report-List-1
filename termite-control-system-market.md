<p><strong>The Termite Control System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>In-ground Termite Control System</li><li>Above-ground Termite Control System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The termite control system market is primarily categorized into two types: in-ground and above-ground systems. </p><p>In-ground termite control systems are installed around the perimeter of buildings, using a barrier of treated soil to deter termites from entering. These systems are designed for long-term protection. </p><p>Above-ground termite control systems, on the other hand, are installed directly on structures, targeting visible infestations. They typically consist of bait stations that monitor and eliminate termite activity. Both systems effectively safeguard properties from termite damage.</p></p>
