<p><p>The spandex yarn market is categorized into several types based on production methods: Solution Dry Spinning, Solution Wet Spinning, and Others. </p><p>Solution Dry Spinning involves dissolving polyurethane in a solvent, then extruding and drying the fiber, resulting in durable, stretchable yarn. </p><p>Solution Wet Spinning uses a similar process but involves wetting the fibers in a coagulation bath, enhancing softness and elasticity. </p><p>"Others" may include newer or less common methods, contributing to diverse applications in textiles and apparel.</p></p>
