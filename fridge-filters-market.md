<p><strong>The Fridge Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Granular Activated Carbon (GAC) Filters</li><li>Carbon Block Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fridge filter market primarily includes two types: Granular Activated Carbon (GAC) filters and Carbon Block filters. GAC filters utilize loose granules of activated carbon to absorb contaminants, offering a cost-effective solution for improving water taste and smell. In contrast, Carbon Block filters employ compressed carbon, providing a higher level of filtration efficiency by effectively removing a broader range of impurities, including chlorine, sediment, and heavy metals. Both types cater to consumer demand for clean drinking water and better kitchen appliance performance.</p></p>
