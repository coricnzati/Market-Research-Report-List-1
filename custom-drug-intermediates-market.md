<p><p>The Custom Drug Intermediates market comprises two primary segments: GMP (Good Manufacturing Practices) and Non-GMP. GMP intermediates are produced under strict regulatory standards to ensure consistent quality and safety, primarily for pharmaceutical applications. These are essential for drug approval processes. In contrast, Non-GMP intermediates are manufactured with less stringent controls, primarily for research, development, or early-stage projects. While offering flexibility and cost advantages, they may not meet the rigorous requirements for commercial drug manufacturing.</p></p>
