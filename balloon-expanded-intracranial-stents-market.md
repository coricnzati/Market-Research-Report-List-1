<p><strong>The Balloon expanded Intracranial Stents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Loop Stents</li><li>Closed-Loop Stents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The balloon-expanded intracranial stent market features two primary types: open-loop and closed-loop stents. Open-loop stents are designed with a flexible framework that allows for greater adaptability to arterial anatomy, facilitating easier deployment and improved blood flow. In contrast, closed-loop stents feature interconnected structures that provide enhanced structural support and stability within cerebral vessels. Both types are crucial for treating intracranial aneurysms and steno-occlusive diseases, contributing significantly to patient outcomes in neurovascular interventions.</p></p>
