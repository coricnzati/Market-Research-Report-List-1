<p><strong>The Mechanical Fatigue Testing Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Cycle Fatigue Test</li><li>High Cycle Fatigue Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mechanical Fatigue Testing Service market encompasses two primary types: Low Cycle Fatigue (LCF) and High Cycle Fatigue (HCF) testing. LCF focuses on materials subjected to cyclic loading with significant strain, typically occurring in less than 10,000 cycles, often seen in high-stress applications like aerospace and automotive. In contrast, HCF involves materials enduring a high number of cycles, usually exceeding 10,000, under relatively low stress, suitable for components like structures or machinery that experience repetitive loading. Both tests are crucial for assessing material durability and performance.</p></p>
