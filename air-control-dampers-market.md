<p><p>The air control dampers market is categorized into two main types: rectangle and round dampers. Rectangle dampers are widely used in rectangular ducts and offer effective airflow regulation for larger systems. They allow for precise control over air distribution and are commonly found in commercial HVAC applications. On the other hand, round dampers are designed for circular ducts, providing compact and efficient airflow management. They are ideal for residential applications and smaller spaces where efficiency and space-saving solutions are essential.</p></p>
