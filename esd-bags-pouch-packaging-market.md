<p><p>The ESD (Electrostatic Discharge) bags and pouch packaging market is categorized into three main types: conductive, dissipative, and metal packaging. Conductive polymers efficiently dissipate static charges, providing superior protection for sensitive electronic components. Dissipative polymers offer moderate resistance, preventing static build-up while safeguarding electronics. Metal packaging, typically aluminum foil, ensures excellent shielding against both static and electromagnetic interference. Additives can enhance the properties of these materials, improving their ESD capabilities and broadening their applications across various industries.</p></p>
