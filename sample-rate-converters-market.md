<p><p>The sample rate converters market is segmented into two main types: asynchronous and synchronous. Asynchronous converters manage varying sample rates between different systems, allowing for flexible integration and seamless audio playback despite differences in timing. Synchronous converters, on the other hand, work with fixed sample rates, ensuring consistent performance and reduced latency in applications where timing precision is critical. Both types serve specific needs within audio processing, telecommunications, and broadcasting, catering to distinct market demands.</p></p>
