<p><strong>The Quality Assurance Dosimeters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>TLD Dosimeters</li><li>OSL Dosimeters</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Quality Assurance Dosimeters market is divided into three main types: TLD dosimeters, OSL dosimeters, and others. TLD (Thermoluminescent) dosimeters use specific materials to store and later release absorbed radiation as light when heated, offering precise dose measurements. OSL (Optically Stimulated Luminescence) dosimeters use a similar principle but are stimulated by light instead of heat, enabling real-time monitoring. The "Others" category includes advanced dosimeter technologies, such as RFID-based and silicon-based systems, catering to diverse applications in radiation measurement and safety.</p></p>
