<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The AC Voltmeters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Average Reading AC Voltmeters</li><li>Peak Reading AC Voltmeters</li><li>True RMS Reading AC Voltmeters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The AC voltmeters market can be categorized into three main types: Average Reading AC Voltmeters, which measure the average voltage over a cycle; Peak Reading AC Voltmeters, which capture the highest voltage peak within a cycle; and True RMS Reading AC Voltmeters, which provide accurate readings of varying waveforms by calculating the root mean square value. Each type serves specific applications in electrical testing, with True RMS voltmeters being preferred for non-sinusoidal waveforms and varying loads, ensuring precise measurements.</p></p>
