<p><p>The specialty fats market includes various types tailored for specific applications. CBE (Cocoa Butter Equivalent) and CBS (Cocoa Butter Substitute) are used in chocolate and confectionery. CBR (Cocoa Butter Replacement) serves similar functions. MFR (Milk Fat Replacement) enhances dairy products, while CBI (Cocoa Butter Improver) improves texture. Margarine and shortening are popular in baking and cooking, providing moisture and texture. Frying oil is used for deep frying, while spraying oil enhances baking and food formulation by preventing sticking.</p></p>
