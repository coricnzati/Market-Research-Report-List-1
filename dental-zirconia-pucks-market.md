<p><p>Dental zirconia pucks are categorized into three types based on translucency levels: Standard Translucency, High Translucency, and Ultra High Translucency. Standard Translucency offers basic aesthetics and strength, suitable for posterior restorations. High Translucency enhances light transmission, making it ideal for anterior applications where aesthetics are crucial. Ultra High Translucency provides superior aesthetic qualities and is used for highly esthetic restorations, allowing for more natural appearances. Each type serves specific clinical requirements and patient preferences in restorative dentistry.</p></p>
