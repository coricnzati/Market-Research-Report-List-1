<p><p>The scandium sputtering target market comprises two main types: rotary and non-rotatable targets. Rotary targets involve a cylindrical design that allows for continuous rotation during the sputtering process, ensuring even material deposition and enhanced target utilization. In contrast, non-rotatable targets are fixed and rely on localized sputtering, which may lead to uneven wear and limited efficiency. Both types are essential for various applications in electronics, optical coatings, and advanced material production, catering to differing operational requirements and performance metrics.</p></p>
