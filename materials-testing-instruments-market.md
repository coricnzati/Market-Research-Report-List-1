<p><p>The materials testing instruments market is categorized into four main types: </p><p>1. **Solid Testing Instruments** evaluate properties like tensile strength and hardness for solid materials. </p><p>2. **Colloids Testing Instruments** measure stability, viscosity, and particle size in colloidal systems. </p><p>3. **Powder Testing Instruments** assess flowability, compressibility, and particle distribution in powders. </p><p>4. **Liquid Testing Instruments** analyze viscosity, density, and pH levels in liquid samples. </p><p>These instruments are crucial for ensuring material quality and performance across various industries.</p></p>
