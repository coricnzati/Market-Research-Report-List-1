<p><p>The Micro Electromechanical System Oscillators (MEMS) market includes various types: Simple Packaged MEMS Oscillators (SPMO) offer basic functionality in small packages, while Temperature-Compensated MEMS Oscillators (TCMO) stabilize frequency against temperature variations. Voltage-Controlled MEMS Oscillators (VCMO) allow frequency adjustments through voltage input. Frequency Select MEMS Oscillators (FSMO) enable selection between multiple frequencies. Digitally Controlled MEMS Oscillators (DCMO) incorporate digital tuning for precision, and Spread-Spectrum MEMS Oscillators (SSMO) provide enhanced performance in reducing electromagnetic interference through frequency hopping.</p></p>
