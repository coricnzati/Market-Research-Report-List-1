<p><p>The traction lead-acid batteries market primarily consists of three types: Valve Regulated Lead Acid (VRLA) batteries, flooded batteries, and others. VRLA batteries are sealed, maintenance-free, and ideal for varied applications, providing reliability and safety. Flooded batteries, which require regular maintenance and electrolyte monitoring, are commonly used in heavy-duty applications due to their robustness. The "others" category encompasses specialized lead-acid batteries tailored for specific uses, such as gel and AGM batteries, catering to diverse industries like transportation and material handling.</p></p>
